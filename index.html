Why are we building Chipi?

Because the Cloud shouldn’t be this hard!

The Cloud was born as an act of freedom, get away from your desk, access your data from everywhere, collaborate with your team, go and work from the beach!

It was easy and fun. Not anymore...

With an ever growing messy cloud, more and more apps are coming into our everyday work.

We are drowned under tons of information, notifications, endless clicks here and there to get anything done.

Finding anything has become a mission and even the most trivial tasks become hard.

It's time to move on and bring the fun back to the Cloud.
